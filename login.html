How VIP access works (manual for now)

VIP perks and the VIP shop are unlocked using Discord roles. This avoids shared passwords and makes it possible to remove access when VIP ends.

Step-by-step

  1. Subscribe on the VIP page (PayPal handles the recurring membership).
  2. Join Discord: discord.com/invite/2YxYYktH9E
  3. Open a ticket or message a moderator with the PayPal email used for your subscription.
  4. After verification, your VIP role unlocks the VIP channels + VIP shop access.

If you’re already VIP and don’t have access yet, it just means verification hasn’t happened for your account.

Where to shop right now
