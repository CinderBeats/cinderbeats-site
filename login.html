How VIP Access Works

VIP access is managed manually through Discord to prevent password sharing and to ensure access ends when memberships end.

  1. Join a VIP tier on the VIP page.
  2. Join the Discord server.
  3. Open a ticket or message a moderator with the PayPal email used for your subscription.
  4. Once verified, your VIP Discord role unlocks all VIP perks.

Website-based VIP login and store access will be added later. For now, Discord is the source of truth for VIP access.
