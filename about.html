About

Hi, luvs — I’m Cinder Beats.

I’m a part-time streamer and a writer/producer of Dead by Daylight–themed music. I’m lyric-first — the words come first, every time. I love clever wordplay, emotional punchlines, and writing lines that people actually feel.

I also love cosplay, community nights, and creating a space that feels safe. If you’ve ever felt dismissed, judged, or like you had to “perform” to belong — you’re safe with me.

Why I do this

I take care of my mentally impaired sister (since our mom passed), and I stream to make ends meet. But more than that — I stream because community matters. The people here aren’t just viewers. They’re friends. This is a support system. One love.

About the music

I write all of my own lyrics. That’s the work I’m most proud of. I use modern tools to help bring the sound to life — but the heart of every song is the writing. If you’re here for lyrics that are smart, playful, and real… welcome to the campfire.
